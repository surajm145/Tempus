PRE-DEPARTURE SUPPORT

We understand the difficulties students face when they first land in a new country and our pre-departure support helps you overcome all of those to make your stay in the new destination a pleasant one. We’re committed to making sure you feel supported every step of your study abroad journey. Our counsellors are always available to answer your queries and help settle your nerves.

We conduct several pre-departure sessions to prepare you for life in the new country. These pre-departure orientation sessions will help you navigate personal and cultural growth opportunities while you’re abroad. Here are a few things that we will discuss in the sessions.

How do I Accommodation?

Finding a home away from home is an expensive and time-consuming affair. University hostel or private accommodation, we help students take their pick. Our associate partners ensure to find you a perfect private accommodation in the country so that you will feel home at abroad.

Part Time Job and Advice on Internships

Part time jobs can help complement your study and living experience. We’ll discuss how to stay within the rules of your student visa and give you some tips on finding a decent part time job plus, much more practical guidance and support.

What to Pack?

To help you cut back on the packing time, we provide you with a checklist that you can refer to while to organize your luggage

Banking

First things first, open a bank account as soon as you reach your host country so that if you decide to do a part-time job, your employer can deposit your salary into your account. We will provide you with tips to get your finances in order in the new country.

A Peek into The Local Culture

You will be able to interact with your classmates better if you acquire some knowledge of the local customs & culture. We will update you on the activities, culture, & other related information.

Student Life in The New Country

In our session, we will brief you about classroom environments & expectations, different learning styles to help you adjust to life on campus.

How to Deal with Homesickness?

Staying away from one’s family & friends is hard. We will provide you with tips to deal with the feeling of homesickness effectively.
